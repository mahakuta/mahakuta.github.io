Mahakuta - Greater Collaboration

Mahakuta operates at the intersection of science, technology and the culture. ​ Our work focuses on emergent technologies and business models as instruments of social change, and is guided by the values of fairness, openness and inclusivity. ​ Mahakuta is a collaborative enterprise composed of research groups/designers/ that work with both grassroots initiatives and institutional partners.
