Work

Mahakuta operates at the intersection of science, technology and culture.

Our work focuses on emergent technologies & business models as instruments of social change, and is guided by the values of fairness, openness and inclusivity. Mahakuta is a collaborative enterprise composed of research groups/ designers which work with both grassroots initiatives and institutional partners. The collective has a shared attitude of public concern and civic activism, which is manifested in out public research and enterprise agenda.

Story

We started Mahakuta as a platform for Collaboration and Innovation with strong local roots.

We feel there is a huge opportunity to be explored in enabling and empowering the local and rural enterprises. We believe the future for business innovations will happen beyond big cities and towns. We are focussed on creating spaces across India and also enabling intersections in arts, technology and culture. We exist to create opportunities for the next generation of beyond cities in connecting them with knowledge and leaders in the industry.
