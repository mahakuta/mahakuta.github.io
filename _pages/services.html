Learn & Earn

Our vision is an educational system where skills are equated

We intend to work with educational partners on hybrid forms of education where theory and practice come together and openness, solidarity and accessibility are central.

Creative education offers added value because youth learn by doing this, develop a critical positive attitude towards technology and can discover, test, try and express their creativity.

We are in the process of deploying a learning solution for natives in Native Languages. Natives can share their knowledge and also earn. We plan to create maker spaces for learning, doing and earning.
